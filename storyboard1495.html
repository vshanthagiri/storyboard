Curiosities in the Lab
Inside Gru's laboratory, a whirlwind of eccentric gadgets and bright colors fill the frame. The Minions, with their exaggerated smiles and wide-eyed curiosity, gather around a bizarre teleportation device pulsating with lights. One Minion, Bob, is fiddling with a lever, while Kevin leans curiously, his goggles comically askew, and Stuart balances a beaker on his head. The lab is cluttered yet vibrant, with science posters and colorful bubbling potions creating a playful atmosphere.
